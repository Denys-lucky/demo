Looking for some online fun with friends and family, or even people from all over the world? Then mapaboX is the choice for you.
mapaboX is playable by 2, 3, or 4 people.
It doesn’t even matter if you don’t all speak the same language.
The game consists of 10 mapabo (rounds). Avg. game time is 10-20 min.

How to play

The game consists of 10 mapabo (rounds) in which players will be given one question with 10 possible answers. During each mapabo, players take turns providing answers until either all answers have been revealed OR all players have been knocked out. When a player gets an answer wrong, they lose ALL points scored during this mapabo. Players can always ‘pass’ on their turn, thus keeping all their accumulated points. However, they do not get to participate for the remainder of the mapabo. Hint: it’s always worth trying to answer at least your first question, even if it’s just a guess, as you have nothing to lose! The 10th mapabo is mapaboX. All points in mapaboX are double! Player after mapaboX with the most points is the winner!
